Wheelie Fun Fest

SCC’s biggest event of the year. Kids take to the course on bikes, trikes, and scooters to raise funds for the school — and have an absolute blast doing it.

Get Involved

How to Participate

🚲 Ride

Kids ages 2–7 take to the course on their wheels of choice — bikes, trikes, scooters, balance bikes, or whatever they’ve got. Every rider raises funds for SCC.

🙌 Volunteer

Families are encouraged to volunteer at Wheelie Fun Fest. It’s one of the two fundraiser options in our enrollment commitment — and one of the most fun days of the school year.

🤝 Sponsor

Local businesses are invited to sponsor the event. Past sponsors have included Cook’s Corner, Irvine Park Railroad, OC Zoo, and more. Contact FoSCC to learn about sponsorship opportunities.

Next Event

Stay Tuned for Dates

Event details for the next Wheelie Fun Fest will be announced via the Friends of SCC Facebook page and through the school. Enrolled families will receive details directly from the school as the event approaches.
