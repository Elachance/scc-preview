Extended Care

After-School Care

When the school day ends, the canyon is still open. SCC’s after-school program gives TK through 1st-grade children a calm, nature-filled afternoon before pickup.

The Basics

Hours & Availability

After-school care runs Monday through Friday, 2:00–5:00 PM and is available for children ages 4–6 (TK, Kindergarten, and 1st grade) through the day before their 7th birthday.

Transportation available: We offer pickup from Portola Hills Elementary and Trabuco Elementary on Tuesdays and Thursdays. Contact us to confirm current availability and scheduling.

A Typical Afternoon

What the Afternoon Looks Like

After-school at SCC follows a simple, unhurried rhythm that gives kids room to decompress and move after a structured school day.

Snack

Children settle in with an afternoon snack before heading outside. The canyon air has a way of working up an appetite.

Outside Exploration

Free play in the yards — the animals, the climbing structures, the garden, and whatever the afternoon brings. No screens, no schedules.

Hike

Afternoons often end with a walk along the trail. Kids who spent the morning sitting at desks get to move, observe, and breathe in the canyon.

Getting Started

How to Enroll

After-school care enrollment follows the same process as our preschool program. If your child is already enrolled at SCC, contact us directly to add after-school to your schedule. New families should start with a campus tour.
