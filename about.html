Our Story

Founded in the Canyon

Founded in 1990, the Silverado Children’s Center (SCC) is nestled in the canyons of Silverado. We’re a licensed childcare center with qualified early childhood educators, operating as a program of the Silverado Modjeska Recreation and Parks District (SMRPD).

What We Believe

Mission & Philosophy

Our Mission

SCC prepares children to be creative, adventurous, collaborative, and connected to the natural environment — developing independence, teamwork, problem solving, and school-readiness through a nature-based approach.

Our Philosophy

We use an Emergent Curriculum — built around each child’s natural interests and curiosity. When a teacher notices a spark of interest, they follow that spark and build the learning experience around it.

The Space

Our Classroom

Our classroom is both indoors and outdoors. Every day begins with a morning hike through the canyon — along Bobcat Creek, up to the Hawk Tree, through the Stables, or out to the trails behind the school.
