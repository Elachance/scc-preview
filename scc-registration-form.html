📋 Registration Agreement Terms

Please read each policy and check the box to confirm you understand and agree.

Non-Refundable Registration Fee
The $175 initial registration fee is non-refundable. Annual renewal fee is $100.
Tuition Payment Policy
Tuition is due on the first day of enrollment (prorated for the first month) and on the 1st of every month thereafter. Accepted via check or Zelle. Tuition is a consistent monthly amount regardless of days attended — no proration for illness, absences, vacations, school closures, or holidays. Payments not received by the 5th incur a $50 late fee. Accounts unpaid by the 10th may result in suspension or termination.
Returned Check Policy
A $50 fee will be assessed for all returned checks. After two NSF checks, all future payments must be made by cashier's check, money order, or Zelle.
Absences
Tuition remains the same regardless of attendance. No credit is given for absences due to illness, holidays, school closures, or personal situations. Children may not switch to a different day of the week to make up a missed day.
Schedule Changes
SCC requires at least two weeks' written notice prior to changing your child's schedule (days or hours). New schedules take effect on the 1st of the following month.
Withdrawals
SCC requires at least two weeks' written notice prior to withdrawing your child. No refunds will be given without two weeks' notice. Tuition must be paid in full for the final week.
Termination of Agreement
All children are accepted on a trial basis. SCC reserves the right to terminate enrollment without notice if staff determine the program is not meeting a child's needs, or for reasons including:
  • Verbal or physical abuse toward staff, children, or anyone on site
  • Excessive aggressive or inappropriate behavior that endangers others
  • Non-payment of tuition
  • Inability to adjust to the SCC program
  • Consistent or excessive late pick-up or disregard for school policies
Family Discount
Discount is applied to the lowest monthly rate: 10% off 2nd child, 15% off 3rd child, 20% off 4th child.
Department of Licensing
The Department of Licensing shall have the authority to interview children or staff, inspect and audit child or facility records without prior permission, and observe the physical condition of a child.
