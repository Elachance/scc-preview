Health & Safety

Vaccine Requirements

California state law requires all children enrolled in a licensed childcare program to be fully vaccinated. Here’s what you need to know before your child’s first day.

Important: California law does not allow us to accept Personal Belief Exemptions or children who are not vaccinated on the standard schedule. All children must have a current vaccination record or medical exemption on file before the first day of attendance.

Required Immunizations

What Vaccines Are Required?

The State of California requires specific immunizations for all children attending licensed preschool and childcare programs. The full and current list of required vaccines is maintained by the California Department of Public Health.

Medical Exemptions

Medical exemptions are accepted in place of specific vaccines when a licensed physician has determined that a vaccine is medically contraindicated for a child. The requirements for medical exemptions are outlined by the state:

View Medical Exemption Requirements →

Please note: This applies to both medical exemptions and “delayed schedules.” We are not able to accept a doctor’s note for either situation — exemptions must be recorded in the CAIR-ME website (California Immunization Registry — Medical Exemptions) before enrollment can be finalized.

What to Submit

How to Submit Your Child’s Records

Please include your child’s vaccination record with your enrollment paperwork. Records can be brought to your campus tour or your child’s first day.

If you have questions about your child’s vaccination status or records, your child’s pediatrician is your best resource.

Questions?

We’re Happy to Help

If you have questions about vaccine requirements or aren’t sure whether your child’s records are in order, please don’t hesitate to reach out before scheduling your tour.
