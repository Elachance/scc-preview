Come Visit

Schedule a Tour

The best way to understand SCC is to experience it. Come meet the teachers, explore the yards, say hello to the animals, and see for yourself why families drive from across the canyon to be here.

What to Expect

Your Tour at SCC

Tours run approximately 30 minutes and are limited to 2 adults — this is a parent/guardian visit only. Children are not permitted on tours. You’ll get to see both play yards, meet the animals, walk the hiking trail, and ask any questions you have about our programs and schedule.

Tours are offered based on availability. We’ll follow up within one business day to confirm a time that works for you.

Next Steps

The Path to Enrollment

After your tour, here’s how the process works:

  1. Parent tour — 30 minutes, adults only
  2. Child visit — 30 minutes at 11:00 AM, your child joins us on campus
  3. Register online & pay the fee — complete the registration form and submit your $175 registration fee (non-refundable)
  4. Enrollment packet — once registration is received, we’ll send your full enrollment packet to complete
