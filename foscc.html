FoSCC is a 501(c)(3) charity formed to support the facility, programs, and community of SCC. Every dollar raised goes directly back to the school.

Who We Are

About FoSCC

Friends of Silverado Children’s Center is a parent-led, volunteer-driven organization with a simple mission: support SCC by coordinating family engagement opportunities, volunteer projects, and fundraising to improve the programs and facility.

We invite SCC families and community members to get involved — whether that’s showing up to a work day, sponsoring a fundraiser, or simply shopping at Ralphs with your rewards card.

Annual Fundraisers

Major Events

Annual Event — 1st Saturday of June

🚲 Wheelie Fun Festival

Our largest yearly fundraiser! Kids take to the course on bikes, trikes, and scooters to raise money for SCC. The inaugural event in 2021 raised more than $6,500. The 2nd Annual raised more than $9,000. Held at Silverado Community Center, with sponsors including Cook’s Corner, Irvine Park Railroad, OC Zoo, and Wheel Fun Rentals.

Annual Event — 2nd Weekend of October

🌾 Silverado Country Fair

FoSCC proudly hosts the Raffle and Kid’s Zone at the annual Silverado Country Fair, hosted by the Inter-Canyon League. Families enjoy a perfect weekend in Silverado with crafts, games, raffle items, and community spirit. Past events have raised more than $2,000.

Community Event

🎵 Cook’s and the Kids

Live music, a raffle, and a beautiful afternoon at Cook’s Corner in support of the Friends. Past events have featured The Whiskey Throwbacks, Her Silver Plane, The Brandys, Road Soda Rangers, The Plumes, and the James Kelly Band — and raised over $2,500 per event.

Recent Projects

What Your Dollars Have Funded

November 2024

Wish List Shopping Spree

Water pumps for mud kitchens, blocks, treehouse zip lines and winches, hammer sets, bouncy ball racers, baby dolls, books, crates, a safety mirror, and a pottery wheel.

November 2024

Reggio Conference

Three SCC staff members attended a Reggio Training Conference at IVC — funded by the Friends.

October 2024

Air Purifiers

Two new air purifiers for the classrooms plus an air quality monitor to track smoke, ash, and allergens.

September 2024

Pirate Day Puppet Show

A marionette adventure brought to the kids — because Pirate Day isn’t complete without a little drama!

August 2024

Rolling Outdoor Shelving

New shelving for the Pinecone/Oak Tree yard, bringing supplies to where the action is.

April 2024

Outdoor Sink

The Pinecone/Oak Tree yard now has a functioning outdoor sink — perfect for hand washing, paintbrush rinsing, and more.

April 2024

Solar Eclipse Glasses

Students watched the solar eclipse safely — because the natural world offers the best science lessons.

August 2023

Faux Grass & Shady Oasis

Turned a rough dirt patch into a perfect shady reading and play spot.

August 2023

Animal Signage

New animal signs — including an “in memoriam” wall to honor the four-legged friends we’ve lost. ❤️

July 2023

Shade Structures

The school got four; the Friends got one more and assembled them all. Sweet relief for hot canyon days.

April 2023

60 New Water Bottles

Dozens of unique designs so no two kids in the school have the same bottle — easy to tell apart after washing.

June 2022

New Washer & Dryer

A bit of an upgrade from the original set from the ’80s!
