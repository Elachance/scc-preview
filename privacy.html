Privacy Policy

How Silverado Children’s Center collects, uses, and protects the information you share with us.

Effective date: April 2026  ·  Questions? info@silveradochildrenscenter.com

What Information We Collect

We collect information only when you voluntarily submit it through our website. This happens through two forms:

  • Tour request form — your name, email address, phone number (optional), your child’s age, potty training status, and any notes you choose to include.
  • Registration form — family and child information needed to process enrollment, including contact details, child’s name and date of birth, schedule preferences, emergency contacts, and other fields required for enrollment.

We do not collect information from children directly. All information we receive about children is provided by their parents or guardians.

How We Use Your Information

Information you submit is used solely to:

  • Schedule and confirm campus tours
  • Process enrollment inquiries and registrations
  • Contact you about your child’s enrollment or your visit to SCC

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not use your information to send unsolicited communications unrelated to your enrollment inquiry.

Third-Party Form Processing

Form submissions on this site are processed by Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party service that delivers your submission to us by email. Formspree stores submissions on its servers in accordance with its own privacy policy, which you can review at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

By submitting a form on this site, you acknowledge that your information will be transmitted through and temporarily stored by Formspree.

Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies and does not include any analytics or advertising trackers.

Certain pages on this site (the family area and registration form) use sessionStorage — a browser feature that temporarily stores a login token for the duration of your browser session. This data is stored only in your browser and is automatically cleared when you close your browser tab or window. It is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain enrollment and tour inquiry information for as long as it is relevant to our operations. Formspree retains form submissions in accordance with its own data retention policies.

To request that we delete information you have submitted, contact us at info@silveradochildrenscenter.com or by phone at (714) 649-2214. We will make reasonable efforts to fulfill deletion requests promptly.

California Residents

If you are a California resident, you may have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) regarding your personal information, including the right to know what information we hold about you and the right to request deletion. To exercise these rights, contact us at the information below.

As a small preschool program, SCC falls below the revenue and data volume thresholds that trigger most CCPA compliance obligations, but we are committed to honoring these requests regardless.
