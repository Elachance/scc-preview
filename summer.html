Frequently Asked Questions

Summer Camp FAQs

Who can attend summer camp?

Summer camp is open to children ages 4–6 — both currently enrolled SCC families and new families who have completed a tour and registration. Space is limited, so we encourage families to register early.

What are the hours?

Camp runs Monday through Friday, 8:30 AM to 5:00 PM — the same hours as the regular school year. Half-day and three-quarter-day schedules are also available.

How does enrollment work for summer?

Summer enrollment follows the same process as the regular school year — schedule a tour, complete your registration form, and we'll confirm your spot. If your child is already enrolled at SCC, reach out to us directly to add summer.

Are water activity days announced in advance?

Yes — water days are marked on the schedule above. On those days, please send your child in a swimsuit or with a change of clothes and a towel.

Is summer camp a good fit for children who haven’t attended SCC before?

Absolutely. Summer is a wonderful time to experience SCC for the first time. New families must complete a tour and registration before the first day. Schedule a tour to get started.
