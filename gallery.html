Life at SCC

A Glimpse Into
the Canyons

Every day looks a little different at SCC. Here’s what learning in the canyons actually looks like.

Campus Friends

Meet the Animals

Two goats, a miniature horse, Tank the tortoise, and a leopard gecko call SCC home. Caring for them is part of the curriculum every day.

Hands-On Learning

Learning Through Play

No worksheets here. Mud, paint, dough, and water are our materials. Getting thoroughly messy is encouraged.

Nature & Collaboration

The Stump

Children gathered around a tree stump, arranging sticks, stones, and seed pods into something beautiful. This is emergent curriculum in action.

Where We Learn

Our Campus

Nestled in the canyons of Silverado, the campus blends the built and natural worlds — tire swings, garden beds, the Hawk Tree, and miles of trails just beyond the fence.

Beyond the Fence

Life in the Canyons

Every morning begins with a hike. The canyon is the classroom — creeks, trails, wildlife, and the open sky overhead.
